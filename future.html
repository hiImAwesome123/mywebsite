DIY Fiberglass Swimming Pools

Save thousands by installing your own fiberglass pool with confidence.

Why Choose a Fiberglass Pool?

Fiberglass pools are one of the most durable, low‑maintenance, and cost‑effective pool options available today. They arrive as a single molded shell, ready to be set into the ground making them perfect for DIY installation.

The DIY Advantage

  • Save $10,000 - $30,000 in labor costs
  • Faster installation compared to concrete pools
  • No curing time = swim sooner
  • Smooth, non-porous surface resists algae
  • Long lasting with minimal upkeep

What You Need to Get Started

Installing a fiberglass pool is absolutely doable with the right tools and preparation. Here’s what most DIY installers use:

  • Excavator or skid steer (we can help with this!)
  • Crushed stone or gravel base
  • Laser level for perfect grading
  • Plumbing supplies for returns and skimmer
  • Backfill material
  • Basic hand tools

How the Process Works

  1. Choose your fiberglass shell - size, shape, and features.
  2. Excavate the pool area - dig to the manufacturer’s specs.
  3. Set the gravel base - level and compact.
  4. Lower the pool shell - crane or equipment required.
  5. Level the shell - adjust until perfect.
  6. Plumb the system - returns, skimmer, pump.
  7. Backfill and fill with water - simultaneously for stability.
  8. Finish the patio - concrete, pavers, or decking.
