Why We Recommend Fiberglass Pools

Fiberglass pools offer several important advantages.

Lower Maintenance

The smooth gel-coat surface helps resist algae growth and reduces the amount of chemicals needed.

Long Lifespan

Fiberglass pool shells are extremely durable and designed to last for decades.

Faster Installation

Because fiberglass pools are pre-manufactured, installation can often be completed much faster than concrete pools.

Comfortable Surface

Fiberglass has a smooth finish that is gentle on feet and skin.

Energy Efficient

The non-porous surface helps retain heat and can reduce heating costs.

Cost Effective
